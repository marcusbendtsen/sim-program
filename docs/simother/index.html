Behaviors of others

SIMOTHER

In this project, we will estimate the impact of others' health behaviors on an individual's quality of life.

Study design

A population survey where participants will be asked to respond to questions about their own and others' health behaviors. "Others" is here defined as household members, other family members, friends, and co-workers. Respondents will also be asked to respond to a HRQoL questionnaire.

Estimands

  • The association between past week's alcohol consumption of others' and HRQoL.
  • The association between the number of episodes of heavy drinking of other's over the past month and HRQoL.
  • The association between the number of cigarettes others' smoked the past week and HRQoL.
  • The association between past week's consumption of fruit, vegetables, red meat, processed meat, fish, shellfish, sugary drinks, and candy/snacks of other's and HRQoL.
  • The association between past week's physical activity of others' and HRQoL.
